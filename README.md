# Sous

**Sous** is a cooking app focused on helping people understand *why* recipes work, not just how to follow them.

Most recipe apps tell you what to cook. Sous tries to help you understand what’s actually happening when you cook, so you can get more confident and eventually rely less on strict recipes.

It’s meant to grow with you as you cook more often and start recognizing patterns on your own.

---

## What it does

### Recipes

Browse and search recipes by cuisine, diet, difficulty, or ingredients you already have.

Recipes are presented in a way that encourages understanding, not just execution.

---

### Cooking Insight

This is the core idea behind Sous.

Instead of just listing instructions, Sous explains the reasoning behind them — the “why” behind cooking decisions.

This might include things like:

* how ingredients interact in a dish
* why certain techniques are used at specific stages
* how flavor, texture, and balance are built over time
* patterns that appear across different types of food

The goal is to help you start recognizing these ideas naturally as you cook more.

---

### Culture Explorer

A way to browse recipes by cuisine and explore how different regions approach cooking.

You’ll see how ingredients, techniques, and flavor profiles change across cultures, and what makes each style distinct.

---

### Community

Users can share photos of dishes they’ve made.

There are no followers, no comments, and no social media mechanics — just a simple way to show what you cooked and appreciate what others are making.

## Stack

## Why this exists

Cooking apps usually stop at giving instructions.

Sous is trying to go one step further and help people understand what they’re doing while they cook, so they can gradually build intuition and confidence in the kitchen.

That’s the whole idea.
